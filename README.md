# Data Analytics Portfolio | Excel Dashboards

This repository contains a collection of interactive Excel dashboards designed to transform complex datasets into actionable business insights. These projects demonstrate proficiency in data cleaning, data modeling, and advanced visualization techniques.

---

## 📂 Project 1: Bike Sales Dashboard
**Objective:** To analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior to identify the ideal buyer profile for a bike manufacturer.

### 🔍 Key Insights
* **Demographic Impact:** Middle-aged customers represent the highest conversion demographic.
* **Commute Patterns:** Customers with a commute of 0-1 miles are significantly more likely to purchase a bike.
* **Economic Factors:** Visualized the direct correlation between average income levels and purchase intent across different regions.

### 🛠️ Technical Highlights
* **Dynamic Slicers:** Enabled multi-dimensional filtering by Marital Status, Region, and Education level.
* **Data Categorization:** Implemented logic to group ages into brackets (Adolescent, Middle Age, Old) for better trend analysis.
* **UI/UX:** Designed a clean, professional interface for non-technical stakeholders to explore data effortlessly.

## 📂 Project 2: Coffee Sales Dashboard
**Objective:** A comprehensive 4-year sales analysis (2019-2022) for a global coffee retailer to track revenue growth and customer loyalty.

### 🔍 Key Insights
* **Geographic Performance:** The United States leads in total revenue, followed by Ireland and the United Kingdom.
* **Product Trends:** Real-time tracking of roast types (Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, Robusta) to identify market preferences.
* **Customer Retention:** Identified the Top 5 customers and analyzed the impact of loyalty programs on total sales volume.

### 🛠️ Technical Highlights
* **Advanced Data Modeling:** Consolidated multiple tables (Orders, Customers, Products) using XLOOKUP and INDEX/MATCH.
* **Time-Series Analysis:** Integrated a dynamic Timeline Slicer to allow stakeholders to drill down into specific months or years.
* **Custom Formatting:** Applied a cohesive dark-themed UI to enhance readability and visual appeal.
